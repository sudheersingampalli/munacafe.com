MUNA CAFE

About

Indian Restaurant, Tokyo.

Muna is the best Indian restaurant in Tokyo, Japan. We serve delicious Indian food such as Roti, Naan, Chicken, Prawns, Mutton, Fish and all kinds of vegetable foods cooked by special cooks who are having more than 20 years of experience. With a good quality and service, Muna cafe restaurant is a good place to hangout and have lunch and dinner with friends and family. The main attraction, in this restaurant are the "Roti" and "Naan". Lots of Japanese and Indian customers enjoy our food every lunch and dinner. In addition, Muna offers a vast array of specialty crafted dishes including Samosa, Gulab Jamun and drinks. Muna restaurant also accepts customized orders.
